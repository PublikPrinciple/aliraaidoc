# Getting Started with AliraAI

Welcome to AliraAI! This guide will help you get up and running with our AI-powered teacher assessment platform.

## Overview

AliraAI transforms teacher evaluation through voice-based assessments that provide objective, detailed feedback across four critical teaching domains. Whether you're an individual teacher seeking professional growth, an administrator managing teacher development, or a developer integrating our platform, we've got you covered.

- **Professional Development**: Using results for growth planning

## Before You Begin

### Technical Requirements
- **Browser**: Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Microphone**: Built-in or external microphone for voice assessments
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection
- **Time**: 15-20 minutes for assessments

### Account Requirements
- **Google Account**: For authentication (school or personal)
- **Email Access**: For notifications and account verification
- **Organization Code**: If joining a school or district (optional)
