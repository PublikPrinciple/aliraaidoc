# Welcome to AliraAI Documentation

AliraAI is an AI-powered teacher assessment platform that evaluates teaching competency through voice-based scenario responses, providing objective, consistent, and detailed feedback to support professional growth.

## What is AliraAI?

**Core Purpose**: AI-powered teacher assessment platform that evaluates teaching competency through voice-based scenario responses

**Value Proposition**: Provides objective, consistent, and detailed feedback on teaching skills using advanced AI analysis

**Target Audience**: K-12 teachers, early childhood educators, teacher candidates, educational institutions, teacher training programs

## Key Features

### 🎤 Voice-Based Assessments
- Natural conversation flow using Hume EVI technology
- 15-minute voice-based evaluations
- Real classroom scenario responses
- No multiple-choice limitations

### 🤖 AI-Powered Analysis 
- Claude 3.5 Sonnet AI analysis
- Comprehensive feedback across 4 teaching domains
- Evidence-based rubric aligned with CLASS framework
- Immediate scoring and detailed recommendations

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- Dashboard analytics for individual and institutional insights
- Professional development planning
- Historical progress tracking
- Goal setting and achievement monitoring

### 🔗 Platform Integration
- Google Workspace SSO integration
- Canvas LMS grade passback
- Slack notifications
- Zapier workflow automation

## Assessment Domains

### 🤗 Emotional Support (ES)
Building warm, supportive relationships with students, recognizing emotional cues, and supporting student voice and autonomy.

### 📚 Instructional Support (IS)
Facilitating learning and thinking through effective questioning, quality feedback, scaffolding, and use of visuals and language supports.

### 👶 Child Development (CD)
Understanding how children grow and learn, supporting physical, social-emotional, and cognitive development.

### 🎯 Child Outcomes (CO)
Focusing on student learning and achievement, developing language and literacy, approaches to learning, and cognitive thinking.

## Product Architecture

**Frontend**: Next.js 14 + ShadCN UI Components  
**Voice Processing**: Hume Empathic Voice Interface (EVI)  
**AI Analysis**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet API  
**Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM  
**Authentication**: NextAuth.js with Google OAuth  
**Hosting**: Vercel with edge functions  

## Business Model

- **Individual Plans**: $29/month for unlimited assessments
- **School Plans**: $15/teacher/month (minimum 10 teachers)  
- **District Plans**: Custom pricing with analytics dashboard
- **Enterprise**: White-label solutions, custom integrations
